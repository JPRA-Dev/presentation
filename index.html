Why do I want to become a Developer

by João Andrade

Find Out More

Ever since I was little, the “little bug” of new technologies caught me!

At the time I was already learning how to change the bios by myself and how to format and reinstall windows on different computers (this still in windows 95!).

How did it started?

Since I got Internet, I also started to be very interested in development.

I would go to the library with some friends during school breaks, and the IT technician of the school would let us see him working in languages like C+ and Pascal.

Whas it fun?

When I was 14 years old, we used to unlock our Playstation 3 with some friends through a special code connected to a Texas Graphics calculator.

Later on I got an IT Technician diploma after completing courses that made me gain knowledge of computer hardware, Software and networks.

What happened next?

My way of life is to live fully and invest myself in what makes me feel alive...

When I decided to focus more on music, I realized that the IT part of me never faded away and that I was naturally drawn to do IT Technician jobs in my spare time.

How did it go?

After getting my Sound Engineering diploma, I opened my own music studio and realized that both the music and IT world are connected.

These days, most of the songs are done 100% with a computer, using plugins created by amazing developers that give us the opportunity to explore our creativity like never before.
One of my wishes for the future, when I will be a Senior Full Stack Developer, is to also be able to create my own plugin.

Time for a change?

After moving to Belgium, and with the COVID putting a break in the music business, I started to learn some basics of Java online and after that I had my first opportunity to go for a real Development Diploma.

Last year, I took a 7 months Junior Web Developer course in BeCode, that I loved, and after I did a 3 months internship in another company in Brussels.
There, I learned how to develop not just as a team, knowing how to help a colleague and how to work with them, but also how to get answers by myself, being independent.

How did I find SwitchFully?

After this I continued to improve my knowledge and I found your website online.

Your program looks great and the fact that we start our training with a signed contract is definitely a plus, as provides a certain level or guaranty for future stability.

Conclusion

Development for me is not just a Hobby, is what I want my life to be!
